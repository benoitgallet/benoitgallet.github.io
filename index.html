Research

Postdoctoral scholar in Cybersecurity and Computer Science/Ecoinformatics at Northern Arizona University.

Working on porting Number Theoretic Transform (NTT) operations to GPU Tensor Cores for the Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) Kyber algorithm.

Working on the data management and computing aspects of a tree water and bark beetle stress modeling and monitoring tool using satellite imagery.

Expertise in High Performance Computing (HPC), parallel computing, GPU computing, and algorithms performance optimization.
