Benoit Gallet

PhD Candidate in Informatics and Computing

School of Informatics, Computing and Cyber Systems

Northern Arizona University

Research

Interested in the field of High Performance Computing (HPC), parallelism, and optimization of such programs.

Working on the development and the optimization of parallel applications, particularly designed for the GPU and for heterogeneous architectures (CPU + GPU).

Exploring and projecting to explore recent GPU hardware features such as Tensor Cores and Ray Tracing Cores.

Emphasis on data analysis applications (similarity searches, kNN, clustering).
